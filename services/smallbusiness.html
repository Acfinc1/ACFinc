Services for Small Businesses

Our approach is designed to support small businesses, including those generating annual revenue exceeding five hundred thousand dollars. Services offered to small businesses include:

  • Cloud Database: Secure and scalable storage to manage your data efficiently.
  • Bookkeeping: Accurate financial management to keep your accounts in order.
  • Marketing: Effective strategies to reach and engage your target audience.
  • Websites: Professional, user-friendly websites that drive online sales and customer engagement.

We believe that by providing the tools and support that small businesses need, we can help them not only survive but thrive in the competitive e-commerce world. Let's work together to keep your business competitive and your customers satisfied. Consideration is $60.00 per hour.
