Analysis of Policy Changes

Variables Small Business (Increase) Investors (Increase) Small Business (Decrease) Investors (Decrease)
Interest rate Spending decreases; savings increase due to higher credit card and bank rates. Lucrative to save and invest for a higher ROI. Lower interest rates increase consumer consumption and spending. Investors increase investments and capital purchases.
Minimum wage Labor chooses to work more as cost of leisure rises. Employers hire less and invest in capital. Labor prefers to work less, retaining skills for higher wages. Employers hire more employees.
Tax rate Small business deductions and incentives retain businesses. Investment tax deductions attract investors. Lower taxes increase profit but may reduce public goods budget. May increase foreign investment but reduce public welfare.
Social benefits Increases public satisfaction and standard of living. Boosts public welfare, reducing investor utility. Lowers standard of living and public goods. Creates investment opportunities, increasing investor utility.
